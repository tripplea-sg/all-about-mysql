# MySQL Workshop Collection

Welcome to the MySQL Workshop Collection! This repository serves as a comprehensive collection of materials, exercises, and resources from various MySQL workshops conducted for partners, clients, and internal teams. 

Whether you are a partner, client, or part of our internal team, you’ll find relevant content to help you deepen your understanding of MySQL and apply it to real-world scenarios. These workshops cover a range of topics, from basic concepts to advanced techniques, tailored to different audiences.

---

## Contents

- **Workshop for Partners**: Aimed at strengthening collaboration, focusing on performance optimization, and advanced querying.
- **Workshop for Clients**: Focused on helping clients use MySQL effectively within their projects, including best practices and troubleshooting tips.
- **Internal Team Workshops**: Internal sessions focusing on team upskilling, best practices, and optimizing MySQL configurations for scalability and performance.
